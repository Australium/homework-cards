Anakin Skywalker

Anakin Skywalker was a legendary human male Jedi Knight of the Galactic Republic and the prophesied Chosen One of the Jedi Order

Obi-Wan Kenobi

Obi-Wan Kenobi, also known as Ben Kenobi, was a human male Jedi Master who served on the Jedi High Council during the final years of the Republic Era

Kenan Jarrus

Kanan Jarrus, born Caleb Dume, was a Force-sensitive human male Jedi Knight who survived Order 66 during the Clone Wars
